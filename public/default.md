# Client Discovery Questions

Everything we need to know before we start building your website.

---

## About Your Business

1. What do you want your website to do for you? (Get phone calls, get online bookings, show up on Google, etc.)
2. When someone lands on your site, what should they do first? (Call you, fill out a form, book online, etc.)
3. Why should someone pick you over the other guys?
4. Who is your typical customer? (Homeowners, contractors, property managers, realtors, etc.)
5. Which of these common customer frustrations do you hear most?
   - Not knowing what size dumpster they need
   - Confusion about what they can and can't throw in the dumpster
   - Hidden fees (delivery, overage, fuel surcharges, disposal fees)
   - Unreliable delivery or pickup times
   - Not knowing how long they can keep it
   - Difficulty getting a quick quote or booking online
   - HOA or permit confusion for residential jobs
   - Previous bad experience with another company

---

## Your Brand & Look

6. Do you have a logo, brand colors, or fonts you already use?
7. How do you want your business to come across? (Professional, friendly, no-nonsense, premium, etc.)
8. Do you have photos of your trucks, dumpsters, team, or job sites — or do we need to find images?
9. Do you have any flyers, business cards, or other materials you want the website to match?

---

## Words & Content

10. Who is going to write the text for the site — you, us, or someone else?
11. Do you have customer reviews or testimonials we can use?
12. What are the questions your customers ask you the most? (We'll turn these into a FAQ section that helps you show up in Google and AI search results.)
13. Do you want a blog or news section on your site?
14. Do you have any existing content that works well and should stay?

---

## What the Site Needs to Do

15. Do you need a contact form, online booking, price calculator, or the ability to take payments on the site?
16. Do you want to be able to update the site yourself — like changing prices or adding new service areas?
17. Do you use any other tools we should connect to? (Email marketing, CRM, scheduling software, Google Business Profile, etc.)
18. Do customers need to create accounts or log in for anything?
19. Do you need anything interactive like a dumpster size selector, service area map, or quote builder?

---

## Technical Stuff (We'll Handle This — Just Need to Know)

20. Do you already have a website address (domain) and hosting, or do we need to set that up?
21. Do you have an existing website? If so, what platform is it on? (Wix, WordPress, GoDaddy, etc.)
22. If you have an existing site, do we need to make sure your current Google rankings don't drop when we switch over?
23. Are there any rules or regulations your business has to follow that should show up on the site? (Licensing, permits, disposal regulations, etc.)
24. Do you have Google Analytics or Google Business Profile set up?

---

## Your Competition

25. Who are the 3-5 companies you compete with the most?
26. Are there any competitor websites you think look good? What do you like about them?
27. Are there any competitor websites you think look bad? What bothers you about them?

---

## Where You Work

28. What cities, counties, or zip codes do you serve?
29. Do you have more than one location or yard?
30. Do you want separate pages for each city or area you serve? (This helps you show up when people search for dumpster rental in those specific areas.)

---

## Timeline & Decisions

31. Is there a deadline or reason you need the site done by a certain date?
32. Who makes the final call on the design and content? (Just you, a business partner, spouse, etc.)
33. What is your budget range for this project?
34. Do you have a clear picture of what you're getting for that budget?

---

## After We Launch

35. Do you want us to keep maintaining and updating the site after it goes live?
36. Are you running or planning to run any ads (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.) that would send people to the site?
37. Do you want regular reports on how the site is performing — and how often?
38. Are you interested in showing up in AI search results (like ChatGPT and Google AI) in addition to regular Google?
